Who am I?

I'm a born-and-bred Silicon Valley robotics enthousiast who loves to attend hackathons in and around the bay area while also teaching students about the endless possibilities of robotics. I attended Amador Valley High and have plenty of familiarity with the teaching environment and subject matter of the district. I also teach classes to help up and coming middle schoolers and high schoolers the basics of robotics at an entry level for further pursuit.
