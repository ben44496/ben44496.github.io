Arduino Beginner Course (ARD100)

This 2 week long course will cover the basics of an introductory look into what an Arduino is and how we can use the technology to bring about different projects. We will cover the basics of how an Arduino works as well as some introductory-level CS syntax for coding. This course is perfect for someone who wants to try out robotics but doesn't know where to start!

Arduino Intermediate Course (ARD200)

As a continuation of the ARD100 course, this 2 week course focuses on adding new ideas and bolster familiarity with EE hardware. With more projects and introducing new components, students have a better time knowing why the code and hardware work. This is intended following the ARD100 course.

Scheduling

Arduino Advanced Course (ARD300)

The last of the Arduino series covers everything that is needed to perform as an entry level roboticist. This curriculum is cemented in open project learning and the instructor will guide the student through their own projects. Emphasis will be placed on knowledge of components that often come up in robotics. This course is perfect for anyone who wants to participate in robotics competitions in high school.
